    Executive Summary

    Built for patients, gastroenterologists, and Micro Labs clinical operations.

    Between November 2024 and May 2025, Chethan N led GastroTrack from zero to delivery at Techvaraha Solutions. The platform covered patient symptom tracking, doctor dashboards, admin reporting, and real-world evidence generation for Micro Labs Pharma's GERD therapy portfolio.

    He owned clinical discovery, PRD creation, sprint execution, stakeholder management, QA readiness, client presentations, and go-live preparation while reporting directly to Techvaraha's CEO.

    The Problem

    GERD care in India had high prevalence and low structure.

    Micro Labs wanted a platform that could connect patients, doctors, and clinical ops into one coordinated ecosystem.

    Clinical Foundation

    GERD was translated into product rules, data fields, and workflows.
